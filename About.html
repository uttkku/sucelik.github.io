General surgery, especially gastrointestinal surgery, clinical research, and always research Hello! I am Suleyman Utku Celik. I currently work at the Department of Surgery, Gulhane Training and Research Hospital. I work on clinical practice and research of general surgery. My research interests include surgical training and education, statistics, gastrointestinal surgery, and outcomes following surgical procedures. Data and statistics are my passion. Analyzing the data to figure out the real-world causes behind the numbers, piecing together the 'big picture', and presenting this with a perfect data visualization are an invaluable pleasure.
